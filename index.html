Welcome!

Welcome to 2Profs Ice Cream! We’re a small town maker, bursting to share our scoops to the world. What started as a love affair with creating ice cream that is creamier, more flavorful and distinctive than they everyday brand, has turned into a flourishing business in West Texas. We want Ice Cream to live it’s best life with us. Now, we’re here to share with you!
